About the studio

Steady craft. Clear ownership.

Walnut Rock Technologies is a compact digital studio for teams who prefer substance over slide decks.

We design, build and improve digital products with a bias toward maintainable systems and honest reporting. Engagements stay small enough that context never gets lost.

How engagements run

  • Discovery workshop and success metrics early on
  • Design and build in short, reviewable increments
  • Launch checklist covering performance, analytics and accessibility
  • Optional retainer for iteration, SEO and experimentation

Principles

We write things down. We measure before we decorate. We decline work that needs vanity metrics more than outcomes.

Talk through a brief

Share goals, constraints and timing. We’ll suggest a sensible path.
