Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Walnut Rock Technologies (“we”, “us”) handles personal data collected through this website.

Walnut Rock Technologies operates this website. For privacy questions related to this site, use the contact page.

2. Data we collect

3. Why we use data

We process personal data to respond to enquiries, improve the website, and meet legal obligations where applicable. Legal bases may include contract, legitimate interests, legal obligation and consent where required.

4. Cookies

We may use essential cookies for site operation and optional analytics cookies to understand traffic. Where required, consent will be requested before non-essential cookies are placed.

5. Sharing

We may use trusted processors (for example hosting or analytics providers) under appropriate agreements. We do not sell personal data. We may disclose information where required by law.

6. Retention

Enquiry data is kept only as long as needed to respond and follow up, unless a longer period is required by law or an active project.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to certain processing of your personal data. Contact us via the contact page to exercise these rights.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time.
